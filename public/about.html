1. What is PixelCan't?

PixelCan't is a giant shared canvas where you can change the color of one pixel every 5 minutes. It encourages collaboration!

2. What is the main idea behind PixelCan't?

Since you can place only 1 pixel every 5 minutes, it promotes cooperation with other users to create something bigger together.

3. Can I create anything on PixelCan't?

No, you cannot create offensive or inappropriate images. We encourage you to think carefully before drawing anything.
