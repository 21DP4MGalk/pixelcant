How to Start Playing

1. Visit the Game Website ✓

Click on the PixelCan't website and check out the main screen. Sign up using the "Sign up" button.

2. Register for the Game ✓

Fill in the registration form with your username, email, and password. Then click the "Create account" button and wait for the confirmation email.

3. Log In and Enter the Game ✓

Use your username and password to log into the game by clicking "Login".

4. Explore the Game Grid ✓

Once logged in, you'll see a large pixel grid. You can browse the grid and see what other players have created.

5. Choose Your Pixel Color ✓

Select a color using the color selection buttons located at the bottom. Choose a color you like or one that matches the collaborative image.

6. Place Your Pixel ✓

After selecting a color, click on the grid to place your pixel. You can place only one pixel at a time, and you must wait before placing the next one, encouraging collaboration.

7. Join the Chat ✓

To communicate with other players, use the chat window, where you can send and receive messages. Get involved in the conversation and plan the artwork together!

Prohibited Actions in the Game

The game PixelCan't has some essential rules to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all users:

  • No racism or hate speech is allowed.
  • Do not use offensive language or insult other players.
  • No cheating or use of bots to place pixels.
  • Do not create images that contain inappropriate or offensive content.

Violations may result in a player being banned or removed from the game.
